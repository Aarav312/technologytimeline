Intel 4004

  • Year invented: November 15, 1971
  • Impact on world of technology: It has made engineers customizable so they can use it based on their purpose. First programmable processor. Also have decreased the size and increase performance. The computer size have also decreased by a significant amount as the size of Intel 4004 decreased.
  • Predecessor: System/360
  • Invented by: Federico Faggin, Marcian E. Hoff and Stan Mazor
