Transistor

  • Year invented: 1948
  • Impact on world of technology: Replaced vacuum tubes in computer and decreased size of computer. When used computers had same performance and less in size.
  • Predecessor: Vacuum tubes
  • Invented by: Walter Brattain, John Bardeen, and William Shockley
