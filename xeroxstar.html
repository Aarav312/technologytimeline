XeroxStar

  • Year invented: 1981
  • Impact on world of technology:The Xerox star was the first commercial computer to have a graphical user interface(GUI). The computer also incorporated many standard norms for commercial computers like, bitmapped display, windows, folders, icons, Ethernet networking, file and print servers, and the mouse
